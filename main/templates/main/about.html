Our Story

Founded in 2014 in the textile capital of India, Surat, Gujarat, ABC - Atal Bharat Creation began as a small manufacturing unit with a big dream: to redefine quality standards in the B2B clothing industry.

Today, we have grown into a leading clothing manufacturer with state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated team of skilled professionals, and a client base spanning across India. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for wholesalers and retailers nationwide.

We specialize in manufacturing premium Kurtis, Short Kurtis, Tops, and Jeans, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern manufacturing techniques to deliver products that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

Empowering Fashion Businesses

To be the most trusted B2B clothing manufacturing partner for wholesalers and retailers by consistently delivering superior quality products at competitive prices, with reliable delivery timelines.

We aim to empower fashion businesses of all sizes by providing them access to premium manufacturing capabilities that were traditionally available only to large brands.

Our Vision

Leading the Industry Forward

To become India's most preferred B2B clothing manufacturer by 2030, setting new benchmarks in quality, sustainability, and customer service.

We envision a future where every wholesaler and retailer in India has access to world-class manufacturing capabilities, enabling them to compete with global brands.
